About Us

Comprehensive Industry Insight

At Footwear Today (FT), we offer an extensive platform that encompasses all facets of footwear, leather, and allied industries. From sourcing to manufacturing and promotion, we provide comprehensive insights into every aspect of the business.

Global Reach:

With a diverse community hailing from every corner of the globe, FT connects you with a worldwide network of professionals and enthusiasts. Whether you're a retailer, brand, manufacturer, or part of the backward-linked industry, FT offers a global platform to expand your reach and connect with potential partners and customers.

Promoting Collaboration:

FT fosters a culture of collaboration and knowledge sharing among industry stakeholders. Our platform encourages dialogue and exchange of ideas between business leaders, designers, academicians, policymakers, brands, retailers, marketers, producers, workers, and footwear enthusiasts, creating opportunities for innovation and growth.

Supporting Industry Best Practices:

We are committed to promoting industry best practices and ethical standards across the footwear, bags, and allied industries. By facilitating discussions, sharing insights, and highlighting success stories, FT aims to elevate the industry as a whole and promote sustainable growth.

Leadership Recognition:

FT recognizes and celebrates the contributions of industry leaders who drive innovation, inspire change, and make a positive impact on the footwear, bags, and allied industries in Bangladesh. By showcasing their achievements and initiatives, we aim to inspire the next generation of leaders and promote excellence within the industry.
